# Object of the Game
The object of the game is to surround more "territory" with your stones than your opponent. 
*A point of territory is a surounded empty intersection of the board. 
## Example 
```go
size: 9
black: f9, f8, e8, e7, e6, f6, f5, f4, e3, g3, e2, f2, f1
white: e9, d9, d8, d7, d6, c7, c5, e5, e4, d3, d2, d1, e1
```
^Example from; "Go: A Complete Introduction to the Game," by Cho Chikun and Richard Bozulich.
Black has 28 points of territory and white has 27 points of territory. 
Black wins, ignoring any other factor such as komi or time.
